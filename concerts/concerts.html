UWADE
October 2020
Born in Nigeria and raised in North Carolina, Uwade has had her fair share of musical influences. Though she got her start singing in church choirs, over the years she has ventured into folk, rock, R&B and indie, and she recently featured on the new Fleet Foxes record. Her honey-smooth voice eases the soul, while her emotional lyrics to the longings of the heart. Last summer, Uwade planted her musical roots with her single "Nostalgia", and has so much more to share with the world. She hopes you'll be along for the ride.
NOIA
October 2020
NOIA is the solo project of composer/singer Gisela Fulla. Gisela was born and raised in Barcelona by a family of activists. She moved to NY and began working as a sound designer/mixer and music composer for film. She also started producing music with glitch sound design and traces of traditional Spanish melodies under the name of NOIA, which means “ girl” in catalan. She sings in Spanish, English and Catalan. She has released two records "Hàbits" and "Crisàlida“ via Cascine Records and her songs have reached 4 millions plays on Spotify.
NYAH GRACE
October 2020
Nyah Grace is a teenage prodigy with an old soul, a love of new tech, an astonishing voice, and a knack for telling stories. Her debut album, Honey Coloured, is an instant classic that's primed and ready for 2020.
WAYNE TUCKER
September 2020
Wayne Tucker is a trumpet player, composer, arranger and vocalist based in New York City. He is a 2009 graduate of the Jazz Studies Program at SUNY Purchase under the direction of Todd Coolman. Wayne has performed, toured and recorded with many of today's current jazz stars including Kurt Elling, Al Foster, Dee Dee Bridgewater and Cyrille Theatreaimee, pop stars like Taylor Swift, David Crosby, Elvis Costello, Matt Simons and Eric Hutchinson, R&B singers like Ne-Yo and Gabriel Garzon-Montano, hip hop artists such as Jidenna and Ryan Leslie, and with rock bands like Brass Against.
MASAYA JUNIOR
September 2020
"The Masaya Junior alias is mainly a beatmaking and production-oriented project, which aims to combine the electronic, club, and soul influences which have defined the past 5-10 years of my musical life with the guitar-based dream pop, shoegaze, and ambient music that influenced me when I first started recording music."
TARUNE
August 2020
Tarune is the folk-soul solo project of Giullian Yao Gioiello. He melds layers of bare vocals, musing rhythms, and understated production elements to build a meditative journey His songs are all recorded and produced entirely at his apartment in the East Village, NY.
MORGHAN KENNEDY
July 2020
Outside of music, Morghan is an activist, a recent graduate of NYU and works with several nonprofits specializing in supporting performance artists in underrepresented communities. Morghan's latest single, Aces, is available on all major streaming platforms.
CHAZZY LAKE
July 2020
Based in Brooklyn and Burlington, Vermont, Chazzy Lake is a former member of Bison, a punk-disco project, and a current member of J Bengoy. He recently released a cover of Dolly Parton's "Here I am,” and is donating all proceeds from sales for the song on bandcamp to the Black Trans Coalition Covid-19 Relief Fund. His new album, "Sup, Circumstance."" Featuring the single "Not Afraid of your Crying Eyes," is due for release on Bandcamp august 2020.
DEVOYE
June 2020
"As a multi-instrumentalist, producer and DJ, I strive to intermingle the sensibilities from each aspect of my artistry to bridge connections between creator and consumer, creating an expansive aural experience."

Entranced by the empathic conncections made between individuals and sound, Devoye explores the natural and ethereal through various realms of sound.
